What is — and isn't — publicly available

Address-level voter records are not public. Individual voter registration and party affiliation are held by the San Bernardino County Registrar of Voters under California Elections Code §2194 and are released only to qualifying campaigns, parties, scholars, and journalists for limited uses. This dashboard therefore shows aggregated totals by district, which is the highest resolution legally available to the public.
Aggregates here come from the County's Weekly Report of Registration (jurisdiction-level party breakdown, updated weekly) and the Precinct & District Data Export (precinct-level totals). Both files refresh in data/ when you run refresh.ps1.
